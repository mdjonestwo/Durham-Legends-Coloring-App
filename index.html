About The Author

Michael D. Jones II also known as Jones Michael is a self-taught artist and musician based in Durham, North Carolina. His drive, strengths in communication, and gifts in design led in him to found Growth Frequency in 2018. Growth Frequency is a creative design company that elevates history, health, and habits through music and cartoons. Though his creative work is accessible to all ages and backgrounds, Michael has a particular calling to serve young people in communities of color. He aims to use Growth Frequency as a model of passion-centered entrepreneurship to inspire other creatives to dream, take action, and build a life of abundance.
